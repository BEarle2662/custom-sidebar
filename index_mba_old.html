Application Process

GITAM employs a rigorous selection process that considers a competitive set of criteria, encompassing academic prowess, interpersonal skills, and GBAT scores when reviewing applications.

Eligibility: Candidates must hold a bachelor’s degree with a minimum aggregate of 60% and present a valid score in one of the recognized qualifying exams, including GITAM Business Aptitude Test (GBAT), CAT, MAT, NMAT, CMAT, SNAP, or AP/TG ICET.

Note: GITAM Business Aptitude Test (GBAT) is mandatory if the candidate does not have any of the following scores: CAT/MAT/NMAT/CMAT/SNAP/AP/TG ICET.

Academic Excellence and Curriculum

What are the benefits of case-based pedagogy over traditional methods?

Case-based pedagogy fosters critical thinking, problem-solving, and decision-making by addressing real-world challenges, preparing students for industry scenarios effectively. Here at GBS, we have integrated 50% to 75 % of Harvard/ IIMA cases and over 50% of courses are delivered through this unique methodology that are practiced by top B-schools like Harvard, IIMs.

What is the advantage of a research-infused curriculum?

research-infused curriculum integrates cutting-edge business insights, equipping students with analytical skills and innovative thinking to solve emerging corporate challenges.

How does GITAM’s term-based system and academic rigor help students?

The eight-term system ensures focused, intensive learning, fostering discipline, time management, and adaptability for dynamic business challenges.

What makes GSB’s pedagogy unique?

GSB’s pedagogy includes case-based learning, mandatory immersion (optional international immersion) and internship programs, activity-based approaches, and a curriculum designed by experts from Fortune 500 companies and top business schools.

How many terms are included in the MBA curriculum?

The MBA program consists of eight rigorous terms designed to prepare students for real-world challenges.

How will an immersion program benefit my career?

Immersion programs (optional international immersion in Top B schools in USA, UK etc.) at GSB provide hands-on experience, networking opportunities, and exposure to real-world challenges, enhancing your resume and employability.
